├── quick_build.bat         - Fast .exe builder
└── README.md               - Full documentation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🎯 FIRST TIME SETUP:

   1. Double-click: setup.bat
   2. Wait for setup to complete
   3. Icon will be created automatically!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🚀 QUICKEST WAY TO START:

   1. Double-click: run_lizard.bat
   2. Wait for it to start
   3. Move your mouse to control the lizard!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📦 TO CREATE EXECUTABLE (.exe):

   Option A: Double-click: build_executable.bat
   
   Option B: Double-click: quick_build.bat
   
   Then find: dist\LizardFollower.exe
   
   ⚠️ Note: Building takes 2-3 minutes
   ⚠️ The .exe will be about 10-15 MB
